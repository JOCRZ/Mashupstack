Important Notice

This is to inform all students, teaching staff, and non-teaching staff that a Special Assembly has been scheduled to take place on Friday, 5th January 2026, in the School Auditorium, commencing at 9:30 AM sharp. Students are expected to be in proper and complete school uniform, including identity cards, and are reminded to maintain discipline, silence, and decorum throughout the program. Regular classes will resume immediately after the conclusion of the program. We seek the cooperation of everyone to make this event a success.
