Jujutsu Kaisen: Execution Movie Faces Multiple Delays in India

Jujutsu Kaisen: Execution, a highly anticipated compilation film blending the Shibuya Incident arc with previews of the third season, has seen its Indian release repeatedly postponed.
Initially listed for December 3 on platforms like District by Zomato, the date shifted multiple times amid fan frustration and lack of official distributor confirmation from Medialink or PVR Inox.
Timeline of PostponementsThe film's Indian rollout began with optimism but quickly unraveled due to scheduling flux.Early listings pointed to December 2-3, sparking excitement on Reddit's r/animeindian.
Updates pushed it to December 5, then December 12, with no showtimes appearing on major sites like BookMyShow or PVR.
By mid-December, District app showed December 19 as the latest date, though still unconfirmed and without theater bookings.
