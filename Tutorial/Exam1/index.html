Missing Kids No Main stream Media is Concerend?


Mumbai reports a sharp rise in missing children cases, with 145 minors including 93 girls disappearing between June and December 2025, fueling viral social media claims and public alarm.

Police data shows 82 children went missing from November 1 to December 6, 2025, mostly teenagers (41 girls and 13 boys aged up to 18), alongside younger cases like girls under five and boys under 11. Cases span stations such as Kurla Village, Vakola, Powai, Malwani, and Sakinaka, with monthly breakdowns: June (26 girls), July (25 total), August (19), September (21), October (19), November (24), and December so far (11). Authorities suspect human trafficking but note many involve runaways or family disputes.
