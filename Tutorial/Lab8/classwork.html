The Burden of Eternal Time

Frieren is an elven mage who views time through a vastly different lens than her mortal companions. After the ten-year quest to defeat the Demon King, she realizes that the human lives she once took for granted were fleeting and precious. This realization sparks a new journey to understand the hearts of people she once knew only as fleeting acquaintances.
